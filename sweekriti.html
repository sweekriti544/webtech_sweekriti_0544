90,000 People THE NUMBER OF PEOPLE WE SERVE EACH WEEK

Philabundance is the Delaware Valley’s largest hunger relief organization, acquiring, rescuing and distributing food to 90,000 people weekly in 9 counties in PA and NJ. Of the people Philabundance serves, 30% percent are children and 16% percent are seniors. Others who seek food assistance include people with disabilities, single parents, veterans, students and working class families.

More About Us

25,000,000 Pounds NUMBER OF POUNDS WE DISTRIBUTED IN 2017

Philabundance serves our neighbors in need through our own food distribution programs and a network of more than 350 social service agencies, including soup kitchens and emergency shelters. We can’t fight hunger alone -- we need everyone to collaborate, including individuals, corporations, foundations, the government and other hunger nonprofits. Together, we can end hunger and make nutritious food accessible to all.
